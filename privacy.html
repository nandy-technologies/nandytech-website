Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Nandy Technologies LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Orli: Daily Torah Quotes (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We are committed to minimizing data collection. Orli collects the following information:

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of app functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how you interact with the App, such as features used and session duration.
  • Purchase information: Subscription status is managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

Third-Party Services

Orli uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase validation. RevenueCat may collect anonymous identifiers to track subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: Handles all payment processing for subscriptions. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

Orli offers auto-renewable subscription plans:

  • Monthly Plan: Billed monthly at the price displayed at the time of purchase.
  • Annual Plan: Billed annually at the price displayed at the time of purchase.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your Account Settings on the App Store after purchase.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with our features

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the App and manage subscriptions.

Data Retention

We retain anonymous usage data for as long as necessary to improve the App. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is generally no personal data to delete. If you contact us directly, we will retain your correspondence for a reasonable period to address your inquiry.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Orli is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.
